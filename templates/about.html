About The Gift Hustle

The Gift Hustle is a boutique resin art and handmade gift studio, dedicated to creating thoughtful pieces that feel personal, premium and handcrafted with love.

Every product starts with an idea – a memory, a name, a favorite color, a tiny flower. We hand-pour resin in small batches, embed elements with care, sand, finish and polish each piece so it looks and feels special in your hands.

Whether it's a custom name plate for a new home, a resin frame for a milestone photo, or a tiny keychain that makes you smile every day, we believe gifts should carry emotion – not just price tags.

The Process

Design, pour, cure, sand, polish, pack – all by hand.

The Craft

We use high-quality resin, pigments and botanicals for a premium, long-lasting finish.

The Promise

Every order is treated like a gift we're sending to someone we love.
